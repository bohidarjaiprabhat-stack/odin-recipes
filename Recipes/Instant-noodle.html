Instant Noodle

Description

serve instant noodles to your friends like this and make them happy. these noodles can be made instantly in 2 mins.

Ingredients

  • instant noodles- maggi
  • water
  • masala(of your choice)
  • finely chopped vegetables

Steps

  1. put some water in a pan
  2. boil it in a low fire
  3. drop all the vegetables, noodles and masalas
  4. wait until it all is cooked
